FAQs

Abzorb Dusting Powder
Abzorb T Antifungal Cream

Why I am getting fungal infections?

Fungi usually thrive in moist, warm environments. Fungal Infections of the skin can often develop in damp or sweaty areas like feet, groin and folds of skin that dont get enough airflow.

What is Abzorb Dusting Powder?

Abzorb dusting powder contains clotrimazole 1%. Clotrimazole belongs to a group of medicines called imidazoles,which is an antifungal agent that fights and prevents the occurrence of fungal skin infections.

How does Abzorb Dusting Powder works?

Abzorb Dusting Powder helps by absorbing excess sweat and provides soothing dryness. It contains clotrimazole which is a broad spectrum antifungal that inhibits growth of fungi and helps in reducing the symptoms of fungal infections such as redness and itching.

Who should choose Abzorb Dusting Powder?

Abzorb dusting powder contains clotrimazole 1%. Clotrimazole belongs to a group of medicines called imidazoles,which is an antifungal agent that fights and prevents the occurrence of fungal skin infections.

How to use Abzorb Dusting Powder?

Sprinkle sufficient amounts on the affected areas 2-3 times daily. The affected area should be thoroughly washed and dried before application, especially between the toes. In order to prevent the growth of fungal infections on skin, use Abzorb Dusting Powder daily. The powder may also be dusted inside articles of clothing and footwear which are in contact with the infected area. Recommended generally to be continued for 2-3 weeks.

I have started using Abzorb Dusting Powder. By when can I expect to start seeing the improvements?

Symptoms such as itching or soreness on the skin should improve within a few days. Signs like redness and scaling may take a little longer to disappear. If the symptoms do not improve, seek medical advice.

Can Abzorb Dusting Powder be used on children?

Abzorb Dusting Powder can be used on children under the supervision of a physician and in a dose as directed by the physician. It should be applied for the prescribed period only.

Can it be used by Pregnant and breastfeeding women?

The topical forms of this medicine are relatively safe for use until the first trimester of pregnancy. However, they should be used only if clearly needed and only under the supervision of your doctor. In breastfeeding women, though the effects are not ascertained, it is recommended to consult your doctor before taking the medicine.

What if I miss an application?

Apply as soon as possible. If there has been a long gap and its time for the next dose then skip and apply the next dose as usual. Do not make up for a missed application by applying extra quantity.

What you need to know before you use Abzorb Dusting Powder?

It is a topical treatment. It should not be consumed and kept away from children. Do not use the dusting powder on deep skin wounds or severe burns. Avoid contact with the eyes. If the powder gets into your eyes accidentally, rinse thoroughly with running water and tell your doctor immediately Consult the physician if symptoms persist for more than 7 days. Keep out of the reach and sight of children. Do not use the powder if you have hypersensitivity to any of the components.

What are the possible side effects?

Use of too much Clotrimazole Dusting Powder might cause mild to serious side effects. These include flaking or peeling of the skin, hypersensitivity, tenderness, redness, erythema, and irritation, formation of small bumps, swelling, blister, stinging or burning sensation, contact dermatitis, gastrointestinal disorders, dysuria, and depression. Patients may rarely experience mild burning or irritation immediately after applying the powder. On rare occasions, the patient may find this irritation intolerable and stop treatment. If any of these persist, consult your physician immediately.

Can I use Abzorb Dusting Powder to prevent athlete's foot?

Due to the absorbing property of Abzorb Dusting Powder it can help in the prevention of athletes foot. The fungus is unable to thrive or spread as sweat and moisture is under control. Additionally, it contains clotrimazole,an antifungal agent, which can also help in preventing infection by inhibiting the growth of fungi.

Why I am getting fungal infections?

Fungi usually thrive in moist, warm environments. Fungal Infections on the skin can often develop in damp or sweaty areas such as feet, groin and inbetween the folds of the skin that dont get enough airflow.

What is Terbinafine 1% cream and what is it used for?

Abzorb T antifungal cream contains Terbinafine 1%. Terbinafine 1% Cream contains the active ingredient terbinafine hydrochloride which is an antifungal. It kills fungi, which cause skin infections. Terbinafine 1% cream is used for the local treatment of fungal infections of the skin only.

How to use Abzorb T Antifungal Cream?

Abzorb T Antifungal Cream may be applied 1-2 times daily. Direction of use: Cleanse and dry the affected areas thoroughly and wash your hands. Treatment can be helped by Keeping the affected areas clean by regular washing and carefully drying the areas with clean towels and clothes, and not rubbing or scratching the skin. Unscrew the cap then gently squeeze out a small amount of the cream onto your finger. Apply just enough cream to form a thin layer on the affected skin and surrounding areas. Rub in gently. When used between the toes, buttocks or on the groin, the treated area may be covered with a light, fresh gauze strip, especially at night. Wash your hands.

When will Abzorb T Antifungal cream start showing result?

Infections usually appear to improve within a few days of starting to use the cream. It is important that you use the cream regularly even if the infection has improved. If the symptoms do not improve, seek medical advice.

Can Abzorb T Antifungal cream be used on children?

Abzorb T antifungal cream is not recommended for use on children under the age of 12 years.

What if you forget to use Abzorb T Antifungal cream?

If you forget to use the cream, apply it as soon as you remember. If it is time for the next application, carry on as normal. It is important to try to remember to use the cream or the infection may recur.

What If you use more Abzorb T Antifungal Cream than you should?

Remove some of the cream if you apply more than a thin layer.

What you need to know before you use Abzorb T Antifungal Cream?

Do not use the cream - if you are allergic to terbinafine hydrochloride or any of the other ingredients of this medicine. Warnings and precautions: This cream is for external use only. Avoid contact with the eyes. If the cream gets in your eyes accidentally, rinse thoroughly with running water and tell your doctor immediately. In the event of allergic reaction, stop using the cream. If you or someone else swallows the cream, go to your doctor or nearest hospital emergency department immediately.

Can it be used in Pregnant and breast-feeding women?

If you are pregnant, breast-feeding or are planning to have a baby, ask your doctor for advice before taking this medicine.

Can I drive or use machines after application of Abzorb T Antifungal Cream?

Abzorb T Antifungal Cream should not affect your ability to drive or operate machines

Do I need prescription to buy Abzorb T Antifungal Cream?

Abzorb T Antifungal Cream can be purchased over the counter from a local pharmacy or through e-pharmacy platform without the need of a prescription.
